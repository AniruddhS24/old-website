Aniruddh Sriram

Hi! I am currently an undergraduate studying CS + Math in the Turing Scholars program at The University of Texas at Austin.

I am currently involved in NLP research with Dr. Durrett in the TAUR Lab at UT. My areas of interest within NLP include robust fact-checking systems and summarization methods. More generally, I'm interested in explainable AI and building interpretable models.

Prior to UT, I went to Dulles High School in Sugar Land, TX.

Bloomberg LP

Software Engineering Intern, Summer 2020

(PORT Data Frameworks) Implemented dynamic resource allocation using Apache Spark on Kubernetes for Bloomberg's PIQA data validation engine. Developed a KNN predictor to predict job size and accordingly assign resources. Resulted in a 59% speedup in runtime for our largest data validation job.

Cornerstone Logistics

Co-Founder and CTO, Dec 2021

Cornerstone is my proptech startup that aims to revolutionalize residential homebuilding. Using live pricing data and NLP, general contractors can quickly find materials and labor in their area to build a project estimate. We've raised over 22k in non-dilutive grant funding and are excited for the future of construction tech!

DevRev

Software Engineering Intern, Summer 2021

Designed a word2vec based model in PyTorch to generate vector representations of developers using GitHub commit data. This model analyzes commit strings and file modification patterns to map developers onto an embedding space so that similar developers are "closer" by cosine similarity.
